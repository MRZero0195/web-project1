Bachelor of Information Management (BIM)

Introduction
BIM program provides an intersiciplinary education for student field of Information and Communication(ICT) Technology and Management, with major focus in ICT. Graduates From this program will have the knowledge and skills required to develop business information system,analyze and design databases, develop network Internet applications, and have a sound understanding of business management principles.
